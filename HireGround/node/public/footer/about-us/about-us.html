Who are we ?

We are two passionate students currently pursuing our Pre-MSc at Epitech. Driven by a shared enthusiasm for technology and innovation, we embarked on this exciting journey to create a platform that simplifies the job search process for both candidates and companies.

Our mission is to make job hunting more accessible and aesthetically pleasing. We believe that finding the right opportunity should be straightforward and enjoyable. With our job board, we aim to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers, ensuring that both parties can discover their perfect match effortlessly.

You can get to know us better through a song each of us has chosen, reflecting our personalities and passion for this project. We invite you to listen and connect with us as we share our journey through music.

Meet Bellinna, the designer of this site. She is passionate about aesthetics and am responsible for the front-end development. Always on the lookout for new challenges, she has a great time creating and designing all the pages myself, all while listening to music!

Meet Guillaume, the tech whiz behind this site! Known as the go-to person for all things tech-related, he’s the one you can count on when you need help. With a strong background in back-end development, Guillaume not only took charge of building the server-side functionalities but also lent a helping hand to the front-end team, ensuring a seamless user experience.

What is HireGround ?

HireGround is an innovative job platform designed to help individuals navigate their career paths effectively. We understand that every job is not just a position; it’s a crucial step toward reaching new heights in your professional journey.
Whether you're just starting your career or seeking to advance to the next level, HireGround provides the tools and resources necessary to connect you with employers who appreciate your unique skills and talents. Our platform simplifies the job search process, making it easier for you to find opportunities that align with your aspirations.
With HireGround as your guide, you can confidently climb toward success and achieve your career goals.

Our values

At HireGround, our core values drive everything we do and shape the experiences we provide to our users. We believe in Empowerment, giving individuals the tools and resources they need to take control of their career paths. Integrity is paramount; we are committed to fostering an honest and transparent environment for both job seekers and employers.

We value Diversity and Inclusion, understanding that varied perspectives lead to innovative solutions and a richer community. Collaboration is at the heart of our approach, as we strive to connect talented individuals with employers who recognize and appreciate their unique skills.

Lastly, we are dedicated to Continuous Improvement, always seeking ways to enhance our platform and support our users on their journey to success. Together, these values create a foundation for a thriving job marketplace where everyone can reach new heights.

We are dedicated to enhancing the user experience, continuously improving our platform, and embracing new challenges along the way. Join us as we redefine the job search experience—making it easier, more enjoyable, and tailored to your needs!

Thank you for being part of our journey. We can't wait to help you find your next opportunity!
